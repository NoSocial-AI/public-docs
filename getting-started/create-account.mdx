## Getting Started

Follow these steps to create your NoSocial account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit NoSocial">
    Go to [nosocial.ai](https://nosocial.ai) to begin
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Click the sign-up button and enter your details
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email">
    Check your email and verify your account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile">
    Fill out your profile information
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Requirements

- Valid email address
- Strong password
- Basic profile information

<Tip>
  Use a professional email address for better matching results.
</Tip>
