## Overview

NoSocial is a complete rebuild of the hiring marketplace. We introduce several new concepts in how AI driven marketplaces should operate that improves performance while reducing noise. 

## What Makes NoSocial Different?

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Context & Intent" icon="chart-line">
    All matching is based on context and intent vs keywords or descriptions in documents
  </Card>
  <Card title="Feedback-Driven" icon="robot">
    Every interaction trains the system for better matches
  </Card>
  <Card title="Private by Default" icon="lock">
    Use private information confidently to get results that match your intent.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Market Insights" icon="comments">
    Insights on how your search performs with what is available in the market
  </Card>
  <Card title="Two-Sided Scoring" icon="arrow-right-arrow-left">
    Fair evaluation from both perspectives. Matches are only visible when both sides evaluate
  </Card>
  <Card title="No Social Media" icon="ban">
    No Ad-driven or volume based algorithmic incentives. Social signals are irrelevant. 
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
