## Product status

### Current focus

NoSocial is currently focused on:

- **Roles:**  Product Management, Product Design, Engineering
- **Industries:** Tech / SaaS / Product-led startups and scale-ups
- **Locations:**  Strong concentration in NL
- **User types:** Individual candidates and in-house hiring managers / teams

### Availability and expectations

NoSocial is currently:

- **Access:** Public beta
- **Match volume:** Expect a smaller but higher-quality stream of matches while we grow supply & demand. Our focus is on quality over noise.

### Who should (and should not) use NoSocial today

**Best suited for:**

- **Candidates** in roles looking for intent-based, low-noise matches
- **Hiring managers and founders** hiring for PM and Technical roles in The Netherlands
- **Teams** willing to give feedback on matches so the system can learn.

**Not a good fit yet:**

3rd-party recruitment agencies. Non-tech / non-knowledge-worker roles. Very high-volume, transactional hiring.
