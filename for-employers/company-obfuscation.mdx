## Company Privacy

Learn about company obfuscation and privacy options on NoSocial.

## What is Obfuscation?

Company obfuscation allows employers to hide their company identity until a mutual match occurs.

## Privacy Levels

  <Card title="Partial Obfuscation" icon="eye-slash">

  <Card title="Full Anonymity" icon="mask">

## Benefits

- Reduce unsolicited applications
- Maintain competitive advantage
- Control brand exposure
- Focus on quality matches

## When to Use

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="Competitive Hiring">
