## AI-Powered Evaluations

Learn how NoSocial uses AI to evaluate candidate-job fit.

## How Evaluations Work

<Steps>
  <Step title="Data Analysis">
    AI analyzes candidate profiles and job requirements
  </Step>
  <Step title="Skill Matching">
    Compares skills, experience, and qualifications
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cultural Fit">
    Evaluates work style and cultural alignment
  </Step>
  <Step title="Match Score">
    Generates compatibility score and insights
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Evaluation Criteria

- Technical skills match
- Experience level
- Location compatibility
- Compensation alignment
- Work style preferences
- Cultural fit indicators
