## Create a Job Posting

Learn how to create effective job postings that attract the right candidates.

## Steps to Create

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Employer Dashboard">
    Navigate to your employer portal
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Create Posting">
    Start a new job posting
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Job Details">
    Add role title, description, and requirements
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Criteria">
    Define matching criteria and preferences
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Publish">
    Preview and publish your posting
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Essential Information

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Job Details" icon="briefcase">

  More detailed postings receive higher quality matches.
</Tip>
