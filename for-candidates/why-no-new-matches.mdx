## No New Matches?

If you're not receiving new matches, here are some common reasons and solutions.

## Common Reasons

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Profile Incomplete">
    Ensure all sections of your profile are filled out completely.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Instructions Too Narrow">
    Your criteria might be too specific. Consider broadening your search.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Limited Opportunities">
    There may be fewer openings in your specific area or field.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Profile Needs Update">
    Update your profile regularly to stay active in the matching pool.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Solutions

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Update Profile" icon="pen">
    Refresh your information and instructions
  </Card>
  <Card title="Broaden Criteria" icon="expand">
    Consider more flexible requirements
  </Card>
  <Card title="Check Settings" icon="gear">
    Verify your notification settings
  </Card>
  <Card title="Contact Support" icon="envelope">
    Reach out if issues persist
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
