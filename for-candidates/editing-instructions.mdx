## Update Your Instructions

Learn how to edit and refine your instructions for better matches.

## How to Edit

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Profile">
    Go to your profile settings
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Instructions Section">
    Locate the instructions or preferences area
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make Changes">
    Update your instructions based on your current needs
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Save and the AI will update your matching criteria
  </Step>
</Steps>

## When to Update

- Your preferences change
- You're not getting relevant matches
- You're exploring new opportunities
- Market conditions shift

<Info>
  Changes to instructions typically take effect within 24 hours.
</Info>

## Tips for Effective Updates

- Be specific about what changed
- Add new skills or experiences
- Update location preferences
- Adjust compensation expectations
