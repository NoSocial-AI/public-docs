## For Candidates

Understanding the matching process from a candidate perspective.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete Your Profile">
    Fill out your profile with accurate information
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Your Instructions">
    Tell the AI what you're looking for
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive Matches">
    Get notified when opportunities match your criteria
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Connect">
    Evaluate matches and reach out to employers
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Match Criteria

- Skills and experience
- Location preferences
- Compensation expectations
- Work culture fit
- Role requirements

<Tip>
  Keep your profile updated to receive the most relevant matches.
</Tip>
