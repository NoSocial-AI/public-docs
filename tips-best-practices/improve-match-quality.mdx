## Improving Match Quality

Learn how to optimize your profile and settings for better matches.

## For Candidates

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete Your Profile">
    Fill out all profile sections thoroughly
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write Clear Instructions">
    Be specific about what you're looking for
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Regularly">
    Keep your information current
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Refine">
    Adjust based on match quality
  </Step>
</Steps>

## For Employers

<Steps>
  <Step title="Detailed Job Postings">
    Provide comprehensive role information
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Realistic Criteria">
    Balance must-haves with nice-to-haves
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Feedback">
    Help the AI learn your preferences
  </Step>
  <Step title="Engage Quickly">
    Respond promptly to matches
  </Step>
</Steps>
