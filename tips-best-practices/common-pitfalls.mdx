## Common Pitfalls

Learn about common mistakes and how to avoid them.

## For Candidates

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Incomplete Profile">
    **Problem:** Missing information reduces match quality.
    
    **Solution:** Complete all profile sections and keep them updated.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Vague Instructions">
    **Problem:** Generic instructions lead to irrelevant matches.
    
    **Solution:** Be specific about your requirements and preferences.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Unrealistic Expectations">
    **Problem:** Too narrow criteria limits opportunities.
    
    **Solution:** Balance must-haves with flexibility.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Ignoring Matches">
    **Problem:** Not responding affects future match quality.
    
    **Solution:** Review and respond to matches regularly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## For Employers

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Vague Job Descriptions">
    **Problem:** Unclear postings attract wrong candidates.
    
    **Solution:** Provide detailed, accurate job information.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Unrealistic Requirements">
    **Problem:** Too many requirements reduce candidate pool.
    
    **Solution:** Distinguish between must-haves and nice-to-haves.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Slow Response Times">
    **Problem:** Delays cause candidates to lose interest.
    
    **Solution:** Review and respond to matches promptly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Not Providing Feedback">
    **Problem:** AI can't learn your preferences without feedback.
    
    **Solution:** Rate matches and provide feedback regularly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
  These common mistakes can significantly reduce your success on the platform.
</Warning>
