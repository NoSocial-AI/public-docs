## Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 1, 2025

At NoSocial, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our job matching platform. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site.

<Warning>
  We never sell your personal data to third parties.
</Warning>

## 1. Information We Collect

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="1.1 Personal Information">
    We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the platform, including:
    
    - Name and contact information (email address, phone number)
    - Professional information (work experience, skills, education)
    - LinkedIn profile data (when you choose to import)
    - Job preferences and requirements
    - Company information (for employers)
    - Feedback on matches and candidates
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="1.2 Automatically Collected Information">
    When you access our platform, we automatically collect certain information, including:
    
    - Log data (IP address, browser type, operating system)
    - Usage data (pages visited, time spent, clicks)
    - Device information
    - Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide, operate, and maintain our matching platform
- Match candidates with relevant job opportunities
- Match employers with qualified candidates
- Improve our AI matching algorithms through feedback
- Communicate with you about matches, updates, and platform features
- Analyze usage patterns to improve our service
- Prevent fraud and ensure platform security
- Comply with legal obligations

## 3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="3.1 With Other Users">
    When a mutual match occurs, we share relevant profile information between candidates and employers to facilitate direct communication. You control what information is visible in your profile.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="3.2 Service Providers">
    We may share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as:
    
    - Cloud hosting and storage (Microsoft Azure)
    - Analytics services
    - Communication services
    - Authentication services (Supabase)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="3.3 Legal Requirements">
    We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="3.4 Business Transfers">
    In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## 4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

<Info>
  We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to safeguard your data.
</Info>

## 5. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Your GDPR Rights">
    - **Right to access:** You can request a copy of your personal data
    - **Right to rectification:** You can request correction of inaccurate data
    - **Right to erasure:** You can request deletion of your data
    - **Right to restrict processing:** You can request limitations on data processing
    - **Right to data portability:** You can request transfer of your data
    - **Right to object:** You can object to certain data processing activities
    - **Right to withdraw consent:** You can withdraw consent at any time
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

To exercise these rights, please contact us at **privacy@nosocial.ai**

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and as required by applicable law. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

## 7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our platform and hold certain information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our service.

## 8. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites (such as LinkedIn for authentication). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

## 9. Children's Privacy

<Warning>
  Our platform is not intended for individuals under the age of 18.
</Warning>

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
