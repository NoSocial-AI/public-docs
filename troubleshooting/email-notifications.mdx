## Managing Preferences

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Navigate to account settings
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Notifications">
    Locate the notifications section
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Preferences">
    Select which emails you want to receive
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Frequency">
    Choose how often you receive notifications
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Save your preferences
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Not Receiving Emails">
    - Check spam/junk folder
    - Verify email address is correct
    - Add NoSocial to your safe senders list
    - Check notification settings
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Too Many Emails">
    - Adjust notification frequency
    - Turn off non-essential notifications
    - Use digest mode for batched updates
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  Enable at least new match notifications to stay informed about opportunities.
</Tip>
