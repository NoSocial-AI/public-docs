## Not Getting Matches?

If you're not receiving matches, here's how to troubleshoot and improve your results.

## Diagnostic Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Profile Completeness">
    Verify all required fields are filled out
  </Step>
  <Step title="Instructions Quality">
    Review and refine your matching instructions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Criteria Flexibility">
    Consider if your criteria are too restrictive
  </Step>
  <Step title="Account Status">
    Ensure your account is active and verified
  </Step>
  <Step title="Market Conditions">
    Check if there are opportunities in your field
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Causes

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Profile Issues">
    - Incomplete information
    - Outdated details
    - Missing key skills or requirements
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Instruction Problems">
    - Too specific or narrow
    - Conflicting requirements
    - Unclear preferences
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Market Factors">
    - Limited opportunities in your niche
    - High competition for roles
    - Seasonal hiring patterns
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Technical Issues">
    - Account not verified
    - Privacy settings too restrictive
    - Notification issues
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Solutions
