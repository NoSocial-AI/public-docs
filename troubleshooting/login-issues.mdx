## Login Troubleshooting

Having trouble logging in? Here are common solutions.

## Common Login Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Forgot Password">
    **Solution:** Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the reset instructions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Account Locked">
    **Solution:** Contact support if your account has been locked due to multiple failed login attempts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Email Not Recognized">
    **Solution:** Verify you're using the correct email address. Check for typos.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Verification Email Not Received">
    **Solution:** Check spam folder. Request a new verification email if needed.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Step-by-Step Solutions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Email">
    Verify you're using the correct email address
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset Password">
    Use the password reset feature if needed
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear Cache">
    Clear browser cache and cookies
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try Different Browser">
    Test with another browser
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Support">
    Reach out if issues persist
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Prevention Tips

- Use a strong, memorable password
- Save credentials in a password manager
- Keep your email address updated
- Enable two-factor authentication

<Warning>
  Never share your password with anyone, including support staff.
</Warning>
